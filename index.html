    Google and Microsoft collaborate to build AngularJS 2.0 on TypeScript

    TypeScript 1.5 release in the next few weeks will be the first results of collaboration. TypeScript will absorb advanced features of AtScript like Annotations. Annotations is a way to add metadata to class declarations for use by dependency injection or compilation directives.

    Apple to launch music streaming service in June at WWDC

    The proposed service will be based on Beat's music streaming service but will be iTunes branded. The service will also support major non-Apple platforms like the web and Android. Apple is planning to offer paid subscription at $7.99/month, $2 less than current Spotify and Rdio rates.
